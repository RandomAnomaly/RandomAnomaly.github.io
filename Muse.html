Writing up Muse presentation

17 Sep 2018

Currently writing up my presentation for the Muse / Machine Learning project. Basically I am taking data from a Muse headband, which is a consumer-grade eeg device, and putting the results through a few machine learning algorithms. It's pretty fun, if a little strenuous. Trying to find out whether we can differentiate between high and low cognitive load events. I get subjects to sit at a table wearing the headset, and get them to perform a 'difficult' task, and an 'easy' task. For example, increasingly difficult division problems.
